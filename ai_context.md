# Enterprise ALM - AI Context & Handover Summary

## 1. Primary Goal & Project Overview

### Vision
**Enterprise Asset & License Manager (ALM)** is a comprehensive web-based platform for managing organizational hardware assets and SaaS software licenses at enterprise scale.

### Core Business Objectives
- Track hardware assets (laptops, desktops, servers) with maintenance history and depreciation
- Manage SaaS licenses with seat allocation and renewal tracking
- Assign hardware assets and software licenses to specific employees (Checkout/Return tracking)
- Enforce role-based access control (Admin, Manager, Viewer)
- Provide real-time visibility into inventory via dashboards
- Maintain compliance audit trails for all changes
- Send proactive alerts for license expiry and warranty deadlines
